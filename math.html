About Department

University Department of Mathematics started PG teaching in 1960. The Department is known for quality teaching and research. It is a matter of pride that alumni of the Department are steering some of the Apex institutes of India and abroad. Presently teachers of the Department are working in the field of Integral Transform, Distribution Theory Wavelet analysis, Graph Theory, Number Theory, Operations Research, Bio fluid Dynamics, Modeling, Combinatorics, Genetic Algebra etc. The Department has also started teaching M.C.A from 2003 under self-finance scheme.

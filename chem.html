About Department

The Department of chemistry was established in 1958. The Department provides quality teaching and research facility to the students. A number of alumni of the Department are involved in research work in national and international laboratories. They are serving the nation in various capacites in teaching in central and state services, forest Department services and medical professionals. More than 500 national and international papers have been published by the faculty members since inception of the Department. Till date, around 260 Ph.D. degrees has been awarded to researchers.

Area of Research : Solid State & Environmental Studies.
